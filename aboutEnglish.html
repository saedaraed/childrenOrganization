How are we

The Palestinian Early Childhood Education Programs Association was established in 1973 as a charitable organization with a license and oversight from the Ministries of Interior and Social Development. Where it oversees an integrated system for early childhood, the association is concerned with providing comprehensive, educational, recreational, and high-quality educational services for children under the age of six that comply with international standards to highlight their mental abilities and develop their social and scientific intelligence.

Our message

The association seeks to promote a conscious society by raising and rehabilitating children through modern educational and recreational programs and activities by strengthening communication with supporters and beneficiaries, in an integrated context that takes into account the multiple dimensions of the child's personality.

Our goals

  • Developing the child's mental, linguistic and psychological abilities through the use of communication skills with advanced and integrated educational policies
  • اFor special attention to children with disabilities and the development of their mental and physical abilities, by integrating them into society
  • Raising the educational, educational and psychological capabilities of child educators to achieve the integrated growth of the child
  • Supporting parents and participating in raising their children in the best educational way
